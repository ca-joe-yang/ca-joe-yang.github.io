Short Bio

I am a third-year PhD student at Purdue University, studying for a computer science degree under the supervision of Prof. Raymond A. Yeh.

My research interests include improving pre-trained models with minimal test-time effort and meta-learning. I have also worked on image outpainting (synthesis) and representation learning for image retrieval.

I received my Bachelor's and Master's degrees from National Taiwan University, where I was advised by Prof. Yung-Yu Chuang. Afterward, I worked as a Research Assistant for two years in VLLab under the guidance of Prof. Yu-Chiang Frank Wang.
